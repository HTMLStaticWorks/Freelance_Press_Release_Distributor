Protecting Crisis PR in the Age of Social Media

Navigating brand reputation in a 24/7 news cycle.

In the digital era, a minor issue can become a global crisis in minutes. Preparation is no longer optional; it's a survival requirement.

1. Speed is Everything

The first 60 minutes of a crisis are the most critical. Silence is often interpreted as guilt or incompetence. Having pre-approved response frameworks can save precious time.

2. Radical Transparency

Today's audience values authenticity. Owning the narrative by being transparent about what happened and what you're doing to fix it is the fastest way to restore trust.

"You don't manage a crisis; you manage the response to it."

Robert Vance
