NeoBank Launch: Dominating the Digital Banking Conversation

A multi-phase distribution strategy that secured 450+ global pickups and established NeoBank as a market leader in just 48 hours.

12M+

Total Reach

The Challenge

NeoBank was entering a crowded market with established players. They needed a launch campaign that wouldn't just announce their existence, but would signal a major shift in the industry. The goal was to reach both institutional investors and early-adopting consumers simultaneously.

Our Strategy

We implemented a three-tiered distribution approach:

  1. Exclusive Embargo: Secured a tier-1 exclusive feature with TechCrunch 48 hours prior to launch.
  2. Global Wire Blast: Synchronized distribution across AP and Reuters financial wires at precisely 9:00 AM EST on launch day.
  3. Niche Pitching: Direct outreach to 150+ fintech bloggers and trade publications to ensure depth of coverage.

The Results

450+

News Outlets

12

Tier-1 Placements

85%

Positive Sentiment

The campaign resulted in an immediate 300% spike in waitlist sign-ups and 3 inbound inquiries from major venture capital firms within the first week.

Key Takeaway

Timing and narrative synchronization across multiple channels is the difference between a "noisy launch" and a "market-defining moment."
