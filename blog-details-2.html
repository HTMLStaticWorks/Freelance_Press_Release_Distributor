Beyond Impressions: Measuring PR ROI

Moving from vanity metrics to real business impact.

For years, the PR industry relied on "Estimated Impressions" as its primary metric. But in a data-driven world, CFOs want to see more than just big numbers.

1. Quality over Quantity

One placement in a highly targeted trade publication can often drive more leads than a dozen mentions in general news outlets. Focus on reach within your specific buyer persona.

2. Attribution & Backlinks

Modern PR provides a unique opportunity for SEO. Track the organic backlinks generated by your coverage and monitor their impact on your search rankings and direct referral traffic.
