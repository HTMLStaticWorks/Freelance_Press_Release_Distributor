WirePress was founded by a team of ex-journalists and PR-tech engineers with a simple mission: to build the world's most transparent and effective media outreach platform.

The WirePress Story

From a Boutique Agency to a Global Network

What started as a small media relations firm in New York has evolved into a global PR-tech powerhouse. We realized that the traditional "spray and pray" method of PR distribution was broken. Brands were paying thousands for "impressions" that didn't exist.

We built WirePress to provide real-world media value. By combining editorial expertise with AI-driven distribution, we ensure that every announcement has the best possible chance of reaching a journalist's desk and landing a placement.
