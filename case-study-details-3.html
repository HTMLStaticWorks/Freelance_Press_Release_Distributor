Driving Record Attendance for Global Tech Expo 2025

A multi-phase PR campaign that leveraged influencer outreach and tiered wire distribution to sell out the world's largest tech conference.

$1.2M

Ad Value Equivalent

The Goal

Global Tech Expo (GTE) needed to move from being a regional trade show to a global "must-attend" event for the Fortune 500. With only 6 months until the 2025 event, they needed a PR engine that could drive ticket sales and secure high-level media partnerships simultaneously.

The Multi-Phase Rollout

Phase 1: The "Hype" Cycle

Utilized influencer outreach to top tech YouTubers and bloggers to build organic social anticipation 5 months out.

Phase 2: The "Authority" Push

Monthly wire distributions highlighting keynote speakers and major exhibitors to secure editorial calendar inclusions.

Quantifiable Success

The campaign didn't just generate noise; it drove measurable attendee acquisition and speaker interest.

  • 2.5k+ Direct media requests for press passes.
  • 40% Increase in international attendee registration.
  • $1.2M in earned media value across 30+ countries.
